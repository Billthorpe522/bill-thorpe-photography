About

Reading the light,
capturing the moment

Every great photograph starts the same way — with light. Learning how it falls across a face at golden hour, how it cuts through a morning mist over the Kankakee, how it bounces off a Chicago skyscraper at blue hour. That's what I chase.

I enjoy the beauty I see through the lens — whether it's a couple in love, your son hitting his first home run, the first school formal, or anything that needs to be remembered. I put the same effort into every photograph I take. My goal is to give you the best memory to keep forever. Based in Manhattan, Illinois, I'm available for work throughout the Chicago area.
